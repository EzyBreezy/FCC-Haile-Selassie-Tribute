The Emperor Haile Selassie

Here's a time line of Emperor HAile Selassie

  • 1892: On July 23, 1892 Tafari Makonnen Woldemikael born in a village of Ejersa Goro in the Harar province of Ethiopia.
  • 1905: At the age of 13 on November 1 1905 Selassie was given the title "Commander of the gate" roughly equivalent to "count"
  • 1906: on March 21 1906 his father Ras Makonnen died at Kulib
  • 1911: Tafari Makonnen Woldemikael married Wayzaro Menen Asfaw, great granddaughter of Menilek II.
  • 1923: Signs up Ethiopia to membership of the League of nations.
  • 1928: Crowned King of Shewa kingdom
  • 1930: Becomes Emperror Haile Selassie when Empress Zewditu succumbs to diabetes.
  • 1935: Italizan forces cross the border into Ethiopia from Eritrea using aerial warfare and poison gas, three days after Haile Selassie has left the country on the Imperial Railway.
  • 1936: Haile Selassie arrives in Geneva where he addressed the League of Nations. "It is us today, It will be you tomorrow." He pointedly tells the Gneeral Assembly.
  • 1941: Haile Selassie returns to Ethiopia. Following the italian withdrawl from africa after heavy losses
  • 1942: Britain hands back full sovereignty to Ethiopia.
  • 1963: Selassie held principles of international cooperation and collective responsibility underline his found of the Organisation of African Unity.
  • 1966: Haile Selassie plane is swamped by an enthusiastic crowd as it lands in Jamaica on April 21st, 1966. By those of the Rastafarian religion that bears the Emperor's birth name who believe his visit to be the coming ofthe black messiah. April 21st is still celebrated by Rastafarians as Grounation Day.
  • 1973: Famine in Ethiopia
  • 1974: Selassie is deposed in September 1974 bringin the long-reigning Solomonic dynasty to an end.
  • 1975: Selassie died on August 27, 1975 The cause of death is officially given as respiratory failure, but many believe he was killed by the members of the new military government.
